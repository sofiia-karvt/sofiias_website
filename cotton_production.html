Cotton in Israel

Israel is a pioneer in using cutting-edge technologies for cotton harvesting. The country employs advanced irrigation systems, such as drip irrigation, which conserves water while ensuring optimal plant growth. Additionally, automated harvesting machines equipped with real-time data sensors help maximize yield efficiency by assessing cotton quality directly in the field.

The integration of precision agriculture techniques, including satellite imaging and GPS-guided machinery, allows farmers to monitor crop health, reduce waste, and optimize the use of resources.

Steps of Cotton Production

Step 1: Planting

Seeds are planted using advanced precision sowing machines.

Step 2: Irrigation

Efficient drip irrigation delivers water and nutrients directly to the roots.

Step 3: Growth Monitoring

Satellite and drone technologies track growth progress.

Step 4: Harvesting

Automated harvesters collect cotton with minimal waste.
